Fingerling Potatoes

The Big Snack

Fingerlings hanging out with their friends

Fingerling potatoes are a potato unlike any other. They are so small and finger-shaped. You can eat them for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. You could even eat them in a salad! The only thing you cannot do is eat them raw. That is a mistake that you will regret.

When they aren't being baked into a crispy snack, fingerling potatoes have a wild side. They love extreme sports, Nascar racing, and horror movies. Fingerling potatoes are known to have hot tempers, and are frequently caught in shouting matches and bar fights.

Looking for a special present for the fingerling potato in your life? Fingerlings appreciate a good non-fiction documentary or tickets to a Broadway show. (Their favorite show? Waitress ) They can also be found shopping for hours on Etsy. Remember that while your fingerling is a gentle soul, it is also a fighter, and could probably use new phone case.
